View Calculation Methodology

Formula: IU/hour = Base Rate × UV Factor × Clothing Factor × Skin Factor × Age Factor × UV Quality Factor.

Base Rate: 21,000 IU/hr (conservative).

Clothing Factor: Fixed at 15% (long sleeves/business casual).

UV Factor: Saturation curve to prevent overestimation at high UV levels.

Skin & Age Factors: Adjustments based on the Fitzpatrick scale and decreased production with age.
